Who We Are

Cheap Excuse is Brooklyn-born and battle-tested—where punk meets melody, heartbreak meets defiance, and every chorus feels like a rally cry. We don’t just scream into the void; we sing through it, loud and unfiltered. Our songs are stitched from the scraps of dead-end jobs, late-night subway rides, and the kind of heartbreak that makes you stronger, not softer. We blend raw guitars with soaring hooks, spit lyrics that stick, and build anthems for anyone who's ever felt like an outsider with something to prove. We take pride in writing songs that pack an emotional punch and leaves a mark. Cheap Excuse isn’t nostalgia. It’s now. It’s the sound of Brooklyn basements shaking with belief. If it’s too loud… good.
